Why does this matter?

🧂

Salt overpowers

At 5x, linear salt makes your rub inedible. SpiceScale uses a logarithmic curve to keep it balanced.

🌶️

Heat compounds

Cayenne and dried chilies become exponentially hotter in large batches. We taper them with a power curve.

📐

Sugar & base scale flat

Brown sugar, paprika, and garlic powder are forgiving — they scale linearly. We leave those alone.
